How to play

Your objective is to end the game without losing all your points. If you answer correctly you advance to the next question. If you answer incorrectly you lose 10 points and advance.

You have 4 FIA helps to use, each one makes you lose more points. First help you lose 1 point, second 2 points, third 3 points and fourth 4 points. You start with 25 points in your Superlicense and have 3 questions randomly picked to answer.
